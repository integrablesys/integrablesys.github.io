Introduction

The study of physical phenomena by means of mathematical models often leads to a certain class of nonlinear PDEs known as integrable systems. After more than two years' isolation due to pandemic, this virtual international workshop on integrable system aims at bringing together leading researchers in the fields of integrable systems at offering an overview of some of the new trends and developments in this area, which will provide a platform for researchers to communicate each other and also a good opportunity for junior researchers and graduate students to learn more about integrable systems.

Please note that the workshop is taking place online from 9th to 10th of August 2022.
